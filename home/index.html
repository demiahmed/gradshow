Design Beyond Zero

2022 MArch Thesis Exhibition Review


The SUTD MArch Thesis Exhibition Review is warmly open to the public as a platform for industry, academia and the international design community to see the output of the school and engage in discussion and debate on the future of architecture, our cities, culture and environment.


This year's exhibition theme, Design Beyond Zero, focuses our attention on the urgent challenges of zero carbon and zero waste artchitecture. Beyond this focus, we present synergestic, alternate and polemical projects enriching our understanding of sustainable architecture.



Program Outline

The morning session is open so that our guests may look over the work displayed and discuss with students. During this time an international panel of jury members will be tasked to find exemplary projects in 5 categories: Social Innovation, Future Manufacturing, Design Computation, Research Innovation, Sustainable Environments.

In the afternoon we hold parallel reviews focusing on shortlisted projects and hosted by the panel. We warmly invite you to join these panels and engage in the debate. After jury deliberation, awards will be announced in a closing ceremony followed by a reception.

We welcome you to browse student projects through our Explore Page , which tracks all student projects by the Sustainable Development Goals they have addressed and permits broader exploration of the diverse projects by topic, scale, location, and faculty mentor. Our Projects Page will allow you to see a full list of our students' projects.

We hope that you will enjoy the projects and that we can recruit your support for our next generation of architects as they make their own pathways to creating a better and more sustainable world by design.

Thank you for your participation and we hope you enjoy the day!
